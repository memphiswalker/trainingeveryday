ServSafe Food Handlers Review

A two-hour class for food servers, handlers, receiving and storeroom staff. This focused class reviews the most abused aspects and causes of food borne illnesses. According to the Center for Disease Control and Prevention predictions are one in six people will get sick from food poisoning; 128,000 people will be hospitalized, and 3,000 people will die from food poisoning. Be a model for food safety in your community and assure your customers you do the very best to practice the latest food safety handling practices.

Taken from the 6th Edition of the ServSafe book a review of:

  • Basic Food Safety
  • Personal Hygiene
  • Cross-Contamination and Allergies
  • Time and Temperature Abuse
  • Cleaning and Sanitation

ServSafe Manager Exam Proctoring

Available for managers who are recertifying or prefer to take the on-line exam and need to schedule a proctor as required by the National Restaurant Association. Managers may attend any openly scheduled ServSafe Manager Review class during the last two hours of the class, or, may schedule a date, time and location with a proctor. Proctoring charge is usually $45 per manager. Additional travel charges may apply.

Printed Answer Sheets for the exams are available at an additional charge of $45 per sheet. Please communicate you require one when registering.
