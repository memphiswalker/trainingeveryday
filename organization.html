Tony D’Angelo
President, Break-Through Consulting

Tony started Break-Through Consulting in 1997. Previously, Tony was VP of Human Resources for Denver based Richfield Hospitality Services, an international hotel management company with over 10,000 employees. He worked with Holiday Inns Worldwide during which time he was a member of the corporate start up teams for Embassy Suites and Crowne Plaza hotel brands. His operations experience encompassed being Regional Operations Director for 18 company-managed and franchised hotels.

His consulting services include the areas of Human Resource Management for which he offers a comprehensive HR Audit; Customer Services with a focus on understanding the value of customer loyalty and then training managers on service techniques; Facilitating the development of work processes, best represented by his role in Toyota’s Signature Initiative; and finally Management Coaching.

Jeannie Rasar
Rasar & Associates

Jeannie works with international and domestic organizations with a special emphasis in assisting franchise-based companies develop strategies and execute tactics to support franchisees with operational systems. Her scope of services encompass developing training materials; e-learning; developing operating standards manuals; developing HR systems; instructing management development workshops (specialization in Situational Leadership II).

Clients include J. Christopher’s Restaurant, LLC; Desjoyuaux Pools, USA LLC; InterContinental Hotels Group; Raising Cane’s Restaurants; FOCUS Brands (Franchisor of Cinnabon, Schlotzsky’s, Moe’s Southwest Grill and Carvel Ice Cream); Popeyes Chicken and Biscuits, AFC Enterprises.
